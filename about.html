About

EquityEdge Research, founded by Wyatt Leroux, provides insightful analysis and research on financial markets, and all things finance. Wyatt, an undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo pursuing a Bachelor of Accounting and Financial Management, brings a deep passion for professional accounting and financial markets to the platform. Leveraging his hands-on experience as a Finance Associate at Electrium Mobility and former Finance Intern at Prosperity Solutions, EquityEdge Research delivers high-quality research and analysis to empower investors, professionals, and enthusiasts with valuable information and actionable insights. Join EquityEdge Research on a journey of exploration, analysis, and discovery in the dynamic world of finance.
